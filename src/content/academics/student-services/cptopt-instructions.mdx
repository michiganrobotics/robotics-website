Student visa holders who wish to work in the United States before and after earning their degree can apply for [Curricular Practical Training (CPT)](https://internationalcenter.umich.edu/isss/work/cpt) and [Optional Practice Training (OPT)](https://internationalcenter.umich.edu/isss/work/opt). 

* CPT is for **before** the degree has been completed.   
* OPT is for **after** the degree has been completed.

## Curricular Practical Training (CPT)

Please visit the International Center’s [CPT website](https://internationalcenter.umich.edu/isss/work/cpt) to learn details including eligibility criteria, the types of CPT, important deadlines, etc.

Applying for CPT should be done as soon as possible after receiving an offer letter. The application process should begin with the [Engineering Career Resource Center (ECRC)](https://career.engin.umich.edu/students/international-students/)  for the College of Engineering. The above link will provide the most up-to-date links to the relevant applications and information for both undergraduate and graduate students.

* **Note**: Read the Registration Materials documents very carefully. It will include information and materials needed for the International Center.

As part of the process described in the aforementioned documents, you will need to obtain the **Academic/Faculty Advisor Recommendation Form** **from Robotics**. Please email your advisor at [robotics-sso@umich.edu](mailto:robotics-sso@umich.edu) with a link to the Registration Materials on the page linked above. In that email, include the following:

1. **Official job offer letter**  
   * The letter must include start date, end date, rate of pay and position title.  
2. **A detailed CPT Statement of Intent** \- *For Graduate students only*  
   *  Describe the job you will be doing in detail and give some description of the company and how their work relates to your field of study  
   * Describe how the work that you will be doing relates to the coursework that you have done or will do in your academic program.  
3. *PhD candidates only*: Part 3 of the Academic/Faculty Advisor Recommendation Form completed by your advisor AND a letter from your advisor on official departmental letterhead explaining how this practical training is an integral part of the research for your dissertation.

## Optional Practical Training (OPT)

Please visit the [International Center's OPT website](https://internationalcenter.umich.edu/isss/work/opt) to learn details including eligibility criteria, the types of OPT, important deadlines etc.

As part of the process described on their website, students will need to request an “[OPT recommendation letter](https://internationalcenter.umich.edu/sites/default/files/info/f_optletter.pdf)” from their academic advisor or graduate coordinator.

Instructions for OPT Letter:

1. Apply for graduation.  
2. *MS and PhD students only*: Submit completed [MS Degree Worksheet](http://robotics.umich.edu/ms-degree-worksheet/)  
3. Email robotics-sso@umich.edu to request an OPT letter.  
4. Complete the remaining steps outlined on the International Center’s website.

Note:

* You must be enrolled in courses the semester you apply for OPT.  
* Pay close attention to the processing times.  
* If you apply for OPT and you do not graduate at the end of the semester, it is your responsibility to contact the International Center to cancel your OPT. Failure to contact the International Center can result in you being out of immigration status or the loss of OPT time.
