International students in F-1 and J-1 status can request an extension of their I-20 if additional time is needed to complete their degree.

## Steps to extend your I-20

1. **Determine the reason for your I-20 extension.**  
   Review the International Center's [I-20 Extension Guidelines](https://internationalcenter.umich.edu/isss/current-students/extend-status) to confirm that your reason for requesting an extension is acceptable. Unacceptable reasons for extension can be found on the International Center's [I-20 Extension Guidelines](https://internationalcenter.umich.edu/isss/current-students/extend-status) page.

2. **Email the Robotics Graduate Coordinator.**  
   Email robotics-sso@umich.edu and provide the reason for your delay. The Graduate Coordinator will complete the [Academic/Faculty Advisor Recommendation Form for I-20/DS-2019 Extensions](https://internationalcenter.umich.edu/sites/default/files/forms/I20-DS2019advisor-ext.pdf) and email it to you\*.

   *Note for PhD candidates:* The Graduate Coordinator will email you the completed form after receiving the required advisor letter described in Step 3.

3. **PhD candidates only:** Before the Graduate Coordinator can complete and send the [Academic/Faculty Advisor Recommendation Form for I-20/DS-2019 Extensions](https://internationalcenter.umich.edu/sites/default/files/forms/I20-DS2019advisor-ext.pdf) and funding letter\*, your PhD advisor must email robotics-sso@umich.edu a letter on U-M departmental letterhead that includes:
   * Your expected graduation term
   * A commitment to funding you until degree completion
   * A description of the remaining work and the expected timeline for completion during the requested extension period. This letter should provide a detailed description of progress in the various phases of PhD candidacy towards degree completion, including dissertation research, analysis, writing, defending, and revisions, as appropriate.

4. **Submit all required documents to the International Center.**  
   Once you have received the completed materials from the Graduate Coordinator, submit all required documents to the International Center according to their I-20 extension instructions.

\*Processing note: Please allow a minimum of 2 business days to receive the completed letter or documentation from SSO staff.
